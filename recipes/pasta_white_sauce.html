Pasta with White Sauce

Description

Liven up your pasta with a different kind of sauce!

Ingredients

  • unsalted butter
  • minced garlic
  • all-purpose flour
  • milk
  • pasta of your choice

Steps

  1. Cook pasta and drain.
  2. In a large saucepan, melt butter, add garlic and stir for 1 minute.
  3. Add four and continue stirring.
  4. Boil milk and stir. Add garlic.
  5. Serve with pasta.
