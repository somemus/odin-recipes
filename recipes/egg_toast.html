Egg Toast

Description

Start your morning with protein and carbs. Super tasty!

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Slice of bread
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Vanilla

Steps

  1. Crack your eggs and beat them
  2. Add salt, milk and a drop of vanilla
  3. Soak your sliced bread
  4. In a pan, fry the bread until golden on both sides
  5. Ready to serve
